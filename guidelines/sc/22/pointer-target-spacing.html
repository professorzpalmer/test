Pointer Target Spacing

AA

New

Targets have an area of at least 24 by 24 CSS pixels, except if:

  • Spacing: The offset between adjacent targets is at least 24 CSS pixels, where the offset is measured from the farthest point of one target to the nearest point of each adjacent target including spacing;
  • Inline: The target is in a sentence or block of text;
  • Continuous region: The target is one of a range of contiguous targets within a single focusable region;
  • Essential: A particular presentation of the target is essential to the information being conveyed.

Examples of a targets in a continuous region include the discrete points within a slider, and insertion points in an editable document.
